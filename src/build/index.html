Welcome to the next chapter for babylon.js

The release of version 4.0 marks a huge milestone for the Babylon.js engine and lays the foundation for the future of our platform. It includes major updates like the powerful new scene inspector and the addition of the Ammo.js physics engine. It also features updates to some of our best systems like our PBR rendering and GUI systems. There are also optimizations that will make your scene more efficient like parallel shader compilation and ES6 modules for the engine. And there are many community contributions in this release that unlock new potential like object-based motion blur, trail meshes, STL export, mesh culling, GUI text selection, OBJ loading, new post processes, and updates to the material library. There has never been a better time to see what you can create with Babylon.js!
